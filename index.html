Calculation of area of a rectangle

A rectangle is a 2-dimensional shape/polygon with four sides, four vertices, and four right angles. The two opposite sides in the rectangle are equal and parallel to each other. The area of a rectangle is the space covered by the shape and can be computed by knowing the 2 dimensions, width and height, measured in meters (m). The unit of measure of area is ㎡.
